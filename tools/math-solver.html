Frequently Asked Questions

What types of problems can this solver handle?

Our solver can handle equations, inequalities, calculus problems (derivatives, integrals, limits), trigonometry, matrices, and statistical calculations.

How do I enter mathematical expressions?

Use standard notation: x^2 for x², sqrt(x) for √x, sin(x) for sine, log(x) for logarithm, and e^x for exponential.

Can I see multiple solution methods?

Yes! Click the "Alternative Methods" tab to see different approaches to solving the same problem.
